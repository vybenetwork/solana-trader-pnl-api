Trade tiers (profitable traders, Last 30 Days)

Profitable traders only (ROI % > 0). Six tiers match the trade-count column chart bands (fixed “2” and “3–5” trades, then four merged high bands). Slice size is not “how many traders.” Each trader adds their full tradesCount into their tier; slice % is that tier’s sum as a fraction of all trades from profitable traders—so heavy traders swell their tier. Legend: % of trades, then avg PnL %, realized USD in tier, and trader count.

Volume by profit % (Last 30 Days)

Dollar share per profit band (profit ÷ volume). Each slice is what fraction of total traded dollars falls in that profit range.
